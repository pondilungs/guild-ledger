[b]Guild Ledger[/b] — v0.4.5

You are NOT the hero. You are the guild accountant.

Rent adventurers, send expeditions, collect rent when they die, and grow your guild empire while gold flows in. Every death is a line item. Every quest is a receipt.

[b]How to play[/b]
• Hire party members — passive gold every second
• Send expeditions — burst profit, death risk
• Upgrade the guild — multiply income
• Unlock 6 zones — from Rat Cellar to Celestial Audit
• Prestige — reset for permanent reputation bonuses
• Tap [b]Collect[/b] — 2× income boost (30s on / 30s cooldown)
• Spend prestige in the [b]Reputation Market[/b] — permanent upgrades without hurting your leaderboard rank

[b]Online features[/b]
• Global leaderboard — compete by lifetime reputation
• [b]First to 20 Rep[/b] race — who reaches 20 lifetime reputation first?
• Pick a username and sync your profile automatically

[b]Features[/b]
• Browser idle/incremental RPG — no download
• Turkish & English UI (switch anytime)
• Fullscreen dashboard layout
• Flavor event log — deaths, hires, and profits with personality
• Offline earnings (8h cap)
• Auto-save + tutorial for new players
• Mobile-friendly

[b]v0.4.5[/b] — Balance tweaks, leaderboard race at 20 reputation, prestige shop, and polish.

Feedback and bug reports welcome — we're actively updating!

Controls: Mouse / touch

I just released **Guild Ledger** — a browser idle/incremental RPG where you are NOT the hero. You are the guild accountant.

**New in v0.4.5:**
- Online global leaderboard (lifetime reputation)
- "First to 20 Rep" race
- Turkish & English UI
